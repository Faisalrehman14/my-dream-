User Data Deletion Instructions

Required by Meta Platform — Last updated: May 31, 2026

What data we hold

Wayfair loads Facebook Page Messenger data from Meta's Graph API in your browser session to display your inbox and analytics. We do not permanently store your Facebook messages, profile, or Page content on our servers. Optional webhook delivery may create short-lived server logs (page ID, message metadata) hosted by Railway — these are not sold or shared.

Option A — Delete via Facebook (fastest)

  1. Open Facebook → Settings & privacy → Settings
  2. Go to Apps and Websites
  3. Find Wayfair → Remove
  4. When prompted, you may request deletion of data the app received from Facebook — Meta will notify our server automatically.

This immediately revokes Wayfair's access to your profile, Pages, and messages.

Option B — Request deletion by email

Email alirunyonali@gmail.com with subject Data Deletion Request and include:

  • Your Facebook name or user ID
  • Page name (if applicable)

We confirm deletion of any server-side logs within 30 days.

Deletion status (Facebook-initiated requests)

If Meta provided you a confirmation code after requesting deletion, visit:

/data-deletion/status.html?code=YOUR_CODE (on this website's domain)

Page message history

Conversations on your Facebook Page are controlled by Meta. Delete them in Facebook Page Inbox or Meta Business Suite.
