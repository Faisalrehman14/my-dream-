Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. About Wayfair

Wayfair ("we", "our", "the Service") is a web application that helps businesses manage Facebook Page Messenger conversations, view post engagement, and send customer notifications.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information (public_profile): Your Facebook name and profile picture when you sign in.
  • Page data (pages_show_list): Names and IDs of Facebook Pages you manage.
  • Messages (pages_messaging): Conversation content between your Page and customers, displayed in your inbox so you can reply.
  • Engagement (pages_read_engagement): Your Page posts and metrics such as likes and comments.
  • Utility messages (pages_utility_messaging): Transactional messages you choose to send (e.g. order updates).
  • Page webhooks (pages_manage_metadata): Subscribe your Page to receive real-time Messenger notifications in Wayfair.

3. How We Use Information

We use this data only to provide the Service: display your inbox, let you reply to customers, show engagement analytics, and send utility notifications you initiate. We do not sell personal data.

4. Data Storage

Messages are fetched from Facebook's API and shown in your browser session. Our optional webhook server may log message metadata for delivery — configure it on your own infrastructure. We do not store Facebook passwords.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except as required to operate the Service through Meta's APIs, subject to Meta's Platform Terms.

6. Your Choices & Data Deletion

You can revoke access anytime in Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites.

For data deletion instructions, see our User Data Deletion page or email alirunyonali@gmail.com.

7. Security

We use HTTPS and Facebook's secure OAuth. Page access tokens are used only client-side for API calls authorized by you.
